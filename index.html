Korea-Nigeria Collaboration Guide

Working with Korean Tech Teams

Korean tech companies often follow a hierarchical structure with clear reporting lines. Decision-making may take time as it often requires approval from multiple levels.

Key tips:

  • Respect formal titles and positions, especially in initial communications
  • Be prepared for thorough documentation and process adherence
  • Punctuality is highly valued in both virtual and physical meetings
  • Korean tech teams appreciate detailed technical specifications

Communication Etiquette

Understanding communication styles can prevent misunderstandings and build stronger working relationships.

Key tips:

  • Indirect communication is common; pay attention to non-verbal cues
  • Building personal relationships (Ïù∏Îß•, inmaek) often precedes business
  • Formal language is expected in initial communications
  • Regular status updates are appreciated to maintain alignment
